And did you know that people who play drums differ from unmusical people in their brain structure and function? I read that in a study and don't really know what it means. But I'm banking on it being a good thing. Whatever. Play the drums. Baddum psht!

Video Editing

Editing can make or break a story. It sets the tone, controls the mood and deliberately guides the viewer's emotions through every twist and turn. According to Alfred Hitchcock, "creation is based on an exact science of audience reactions". Editing is a series of tiny, calculated choices that either add to or take away from the journey. A good editor is very empathetic and can tell the difference. Think about that the next time you grab some popcorn!
