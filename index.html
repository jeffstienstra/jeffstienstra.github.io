Grand Valley Automation

UI/Graphic Designer | May 2017 - May 2021

Tasked with overhauling GVA's UI and graphics I pioneered a new position to bring their site designs out of the 90's and into a dynamic, user-friendly and mobile compatible format. See the 'Featured' section of my LinkedIn profile for examples.

Accomplishments:

  • Designed a responsive, intuitive UI directly contributing to increased sales and customer satisfaction.
  • Established the industry standard for BMS site usability in our region based on feedback from customers and potential clients.
  • Improved project effiency and empowered others to make good design choices by developing company-wide template libraries.
  • Researched potential clients to develop unique sales presentations and custom graphics that helped the sales team close more deals.
  • Created training documentation and a custom video tutorial series to teach complex UI and graphics tasks before leaving the company.
  • Led customer trainings with groups of up to 30 on how to access and use their automation sites.
  • Increased consistency between projects by conducting in-house graphics trainings.
  • Due to the effectiveness of my work and the suite of tools I left behind GVA is not planning to hire behind me in the near future until major new features are added to their BMS software.

Stienstra Heating and Cooling

Owner | Nov 2009 - Sept 2015

Inexperienced and fresh out of trade school I was motivated to go on my own after seeing how many hvac companies dealt unfairly with customers. This drive for honest service allowed me to build a successful residential heating and cooling business during the Great Recession while creating jobs for four service technicians.

Accomplishments:

  • Honed my troubleshooting skills and learned to think outside the box.
  • Sharpened my ability to find and sift though detailed technical documentation quickly while talking with customers looking over my shoulder.
  • Planned all marketing and advertising from print and online PPC ads to writing blog posts and attending networking events.
  • Responsible for every customer interaction.
  • Trained new employees.
  • Pushed the limits of multitasking: communicating install details to other techs on site, reviewing technical documentation and answering calls from potential customers all while crawling through a dark, hot insulation-filled attic with a headlamp. Proud to say I did...but I certainly don't miss it. :)
